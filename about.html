Why Do We Join TGC?

We see technology moving much faster than any enterprise, it’s our goal to make sure our students are well aware and educated. So we provide:

INSTRUCTOR-LED TRAINING (ILT)

Instructor-Led Training (ILT) at TGC combines the expertise of our seasoned instructors with an interactive learning environment to deliver comprehensive and effective education. ILT offers a dynamic and engaging approach to learning, allowing students to benefit from real-time interaction, personalized feedback, and hands-on experience.
Our ILT programs cover a wide array of subjects, including technical skills like Linux, Python, cloud computing, and competitive programming in Python, Java, and C. Additionally, we provide training in graphic design, web design, and standard operating procedures (SOP). Each course is meticulously designed to meet industry standards and cater to the specific needs of our students.
The advantage of ILT lies in its interactive nature. Students can ask questions, participate in discussions, and receive immediate clarification on complex topics. This direct engagement not only enhances understanding but also fosters a collaborative learning atmosphere where students can learn from each other’s experiences and perspectives.

VIRTUAL INSTRUCTOR-LED TRAINING (VILT)

Virtual Instructor-Led Training (VILT) at TGC brings the interactive and engaging elements of traditional classroom training to an online platform, providing flexibility and convenience without compromising on quality. Our VILT programs are designed to offer real-time interaction, personalized feedback, and hands-on learning experiences, all from the comfort of your own home.
VILT covers a diverse range of subjects, including technical skills like Linux, Python, cloud computing, and competitive programming in Python, Java, and C. We also offer courses in graphic design, web design, and standard operating procedures (SOP). Each course is carefully crafted to meet industry standards and cater to the specific needs of our students.
The key benefit of VILT is its accessibility. Students can join classes from anywhere, eliminating the need for travel and allowing for a more flexible learning schedule. This format is ideal for working professionals, busy students, or anyone looking to upskill without disrupting their daily routine.

INDUSTRY STANDARD CURRICULUM

At TGC, our Industry Standard Curriculum is designed to provide students with the most relevant and up-to-date knowledge and skills required in today's competitive job market. Our curriculum is meticulously crafted in collaboration with industry experts to ensure it meets the evolving demands of various sectors, including technology, design, and business.
Our comprehensive course offerings cover technical skills such as Linux, Python, cloud computing, and competitive programming in Python, Java, and C. We also provide training in graphic design, web design, and standard operating procedures (SOP). Each course is aligned with industry standards and best practices, ensuring that our students are well-prepared to meet the challenges of their chosen fields.
The Industry Standard Curriculum at TGC emphasizes practical, hands-on learning experiences. Our courses include real-world projects, case studies, and interactive sessions that enable students to apply theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios. This approach helps bridge the gap between classroom learning and on-the-job requirements, making our graduates highly sought after by employers.

Our team is made up of highly dedicated professionals from many technological fields. Our main objective is to bridge the knowledge gap between business and academics via our training services.
